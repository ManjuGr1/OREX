About Us

Orex Technologies was envisioned in 2003 by a group of young software engineers who trusted there capabilities and wanted to make a difference for their customers by bringing in the right technology to their doorstep.
It was in 2018 that these young brains with their unparalleled past experiences brought Orex Technologies to market.
Focusing on building bounding relationships with the clients by value creation the company has significantly grown its customer base across the globe focussing mainly on the Middle East and Asia markets. We helporganizations to transition and transform, to be more efficient and productive. We are known to be in the forefront to bring the competitive-advantage by bringing in products and services which enable our customers to become a high-performance organization in their industry verticals.
Orex Technologies offers wide-range of innovative, integrated applications, and must have solutions to bring the competitive advantage for our valued customers.

We are head quartered at Dubai with offices in Bangalore and Bhubaneswar.
